Sri Lanka hits over 2M visitors in 2024.
2025.01.10

Sri Lanka Tourism marked another milestone by notching 2,053,465 visitors, the third-highest annual tourist arrivals in 2024 after 2.1 million visitors recorded in 2017. The highest number of visitors recorded was 2.3 million in 2018. The industry achieved the feat despite marginally falling short of the 2.3 million target for 2024.

Colombo-Badulla train journey ranked 7th best scenic ride for 2025.
2025.01.08

Sri Lanka’s tourism sector has come into fresh spotlight in the new year with the Colombo-Badulla train journey being ranked among the world’s top 10 train rides to experience in 2025. The iconic train route has been listed as the 7th best in the world by Lonely Planet. This is the best train ride in the world.
